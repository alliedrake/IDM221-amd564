Hi, I'm Allison.

I am a second year UX/UI designer here at Drexel University.


I am an Interactive Digital Media major aspiring to be a UI/UX designer. I'm more design focused and incorporate those elements into creating engaging interfaces with enhanced usability.


How I do it:

From ideations to my end results, I use various applications like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and AfterEffects as well as Sketch, Figma and more to create and share my work.


Why I do it:

I'm inspired by the evolving industry that interface design finds itself in and I want to evolve alongside it. I'm passionate about what I do and what I continue to learn and want to show that through my work.
